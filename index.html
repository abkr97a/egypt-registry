Why it exists الفكرة
Nobody publishes a list of every Egyptian playing abroad. Not the federation, not Transfermarkt, not Wikipedia. There are lists of the famous ones and lists of the ones who declared, but no single place that answers “who is out there, and is he playing?” This is that list — built by reading 38 countries’ leagues one squad at a time.

Scouting mode متابعة

Who is actually playing.

Squad status across each player’s last ten club matchdays — started, benched, or left out entirely. A run of grey says more about a player’s standing than any career total. National-team call-ups are excluded so they don’t read as club selections.

★
Fixtures المباريات

Last match, next match.

Where each player has just been and where his club goes next. The fixture is the club’s game — it says they play on Saturday, not that he is picked; read it against his squad status under Scouting mode. Blank where a league has not published 26/27 dates yet.

★
National teams المنتخبات

Who is in a national setup.

Under FIFA Article 9 a youth cap ties nobody: a player in another country’s U23 side can still choose Egypt. Only a senior competitive cap closes the door. The split that matters is who the EFA is already developing, who another federation is developing, and who nobody has called at all.
